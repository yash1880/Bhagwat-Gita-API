Everything you need to know about the Bhagavad Gita

The Bhagavad Gita contains 18 chapters with a total of 700 shlokas. It is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata and presents a conversation between Lord Krishna and Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

The Bhagavad Gita is considered a celestial song revealed by Lord Krishna to Arjuna. It was composed by Sage Vyasa as part of the Mahabharata epic.

Reading Bhagavad Gita provides mental clarity, stress reduction, better decision-making, and spiritual growth. It teaches Karma Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, and Jnana Yoga for balanced living.

Bhagavad Gita explains four main paths:
• Karma Yoga (Path of Action)
• Bhakti Yoga (Path of Devotion)
• Jnana Yoga (Path of Knowledge)
• Dhyana Yoga (Path of Meditation)
